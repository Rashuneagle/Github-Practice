Tiger Woods

    Life of Tiger Woods
  • Tiger is the son of Earl Woods, a retired lieutenant colonel in the U.S. Army, and his wife, Kultida, a native of Thailand.
  • He grew up in Cypress, California.
  • Tiger played in his first professional tournament in 1992
  • He entered Stanford University in 1994 and in two years he won 10 collegiate events, concluding with the NCAA title.
